Rules for deterministic category identification:


	- Artworks: 
		if words like: (art, work, artwork, cup, photo, painter, photographer, book, opera, ceramic, portrait, paint), then the tweet may be categorized as 
		a tweet about an artwork.

	
	- Festivities:
		if words like: (happy, birthday, happy birthday, celebrating, celebrate, wish, fun, have fun), the the tweet may be categorized as a festivity
		celebration tweet.


	- Promotions:
		if words like: (tickets, check, check out, week, exhibition, explore, [date (current year)], open, opens, don't miss), the the tweet may be categorized 
		as a promotion tweet.


	- History: 
		if words like: (history, [date < 2015], war, past, years, century), the the tweet may be categorized 
		as an historical topic  tweet.


	-On this day:
		if words like: (today, onthisday, [date], tonight, born, die, happen, happens today, happened today), the the tweet may be categorized as a recurrence
		celebration tweet.


	-Vip cit:
		if words like: (said, phrase, citation, famous, [full quoted text]), the the tweet may be categorized as a VIP citation tweet.
